Map

Nurses utilize maps to efficiently navigate hospital layouts, ensuring they can quickly locate patient rooms and essential facilities. These maps also aid in emergency preparedness by helping nurses identify evacuation routes and emergency exits, ensuring the safety of patients and staff during crises.
